About Me

Where analytics, automation, and software meet.

I started my career as a Data Analyst, using Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python for reporting, dashboards, and data analysis. As the business problems I was solving grew more complex, so did my toolkit — I moved into data engineering, building ETL pipelines and automations to keep data moving reliably between systems.

Today, my role also includes business solution development: building internal web applications and workflow automations that dashboards alone can't solve. Across every phase, my approach has stayed the same — understand the business problem first, then decide what to build.
